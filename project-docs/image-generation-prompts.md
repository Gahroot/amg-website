# AMG Website Image Generation Prompts

## Visual Style Reference

These prompts are designed for the Anchor Mill Group website, targeting UHNW (ultra high net worth) individuals and families. The visual style draws from classical luxury marketing — sophisticated, understated, and timeless.

**Core Photography Specifications:**
- **Camera:** Hasselblad H6D-100c or equivalent medium format
- **Lighting:** Dramatic, high-contrast, chiaroscuro technique
- **Color Palette:** Dark backgrounds with gold/sand accents (`#d4c9a8` dark, `#8b7d5e` light)
- **Style:** Hyper-realistic, shallow depth of field, no text or overlays
- **Mood:** Exclusive, protective, guiding, permanent, sophisticated

## 1. Home Hero Section → [PLACEMENT: Home Hero, 21:9 ultra-wide]

### Lighthouse in Storm

**Section Context:** First image visitors see. Establishes the brand metaphor — guidance through chaos. Parallax scroll effect creates subtle movement.

**Image Description:**
A solitary lighthouse standing on a rocky cliff during a dramatic storm. Lightning illuminates the scene, striking the water. The lighthouse beam cuts through dark, turbulent clouds. Composed for ultra-wide format with the lighthouse positioned slightly off-center (rule of thirds). The frame captures the full sweep of turbulent sky above and dark churning sea below. Dramatic contrast between the warm golden light beam and cold dark blues of the storm.

**Aspect Ratio:** 21:9 (ultra-wide panoramic ~2.33:1)

**Specs:** Hasselblad H6D, 24mm wide-angle lens, f/8, high-contrast lighting, compose with negative space on right for potential typography overlay

**Mood:** Resilient, guiding, protective, steadfast

**Composition Notes:** Position lighthouse at left-third of frame. Allow expansive sky and sea to fill the ultra-wide canvas. Golden beam should extend diagonally across the frame.

## 2. How It Works Section → [PLACEMENT: How It Works, 3:4 portrait]

### Golden Compass on Dark Surface

**Section Context:** Appears alongside the 4-step engagement methodology. Illustrates precision, direction, and finding the right path through complex risk landscapes.

**Image Description:**

Composed for portrait orientation with the compass filling the vertical frame. Soft directional lighting from above left creates dramatic shadows across the compass face. The surface texture is visible but subtle. Selective focus on the compass dial with shallow depth of field.

**Aspect Ratio:** 3:4 (portrait ~0.75:1)

**Specs:** Hasselblad H6D, 120mm macro lens, f/4, studio lighting, compose centrally in portrait format

**Mood:** Decisive, traditional, precise, trustworthy

**Composition Notes:** Compass should occupy most of the vertical frame. Fine details of the compass rose and needle should be sharp. Brass and gold tones should glow against the dark surface.

## 3. Home CTA Section → [PLACEMENT: Home CTA, Full viewport background]

### Storm Breaking, Light Emerging

**Section Context:** Final section before contact CTA. Text reads "Turn Chaos Into Control." Image should feel immersive and transformative — the moment clarity arrives.

**Image Description:**
A dramatic cloudscape where dark storm clouds are beginning to part, revealing golden sunlight breaking through. The transition from darkness to light occupies the full frame. Below, a dark landscape with hints of golden illumination on distant peaks. The sense of a storm passing, clarity returning. Full viewport composition creates immersive atmosphere. Text will overlay in lower portion, so upper 2/3 of frame should be the primary visual area.

**Aspect Ratio:** Full viewport (16:9 base, but should work across various viewport ratios)

**Specs:** Hasselblad H6D, 35mm lens, f/11, HDR capture, compose with lighter sky area positioned to not interfere with overlay text

**Mood:** Hopeful, transitional, relief, clarity

**Composition Notes:** Golden break in clouds should draw eye upward. Darker areas in lower portion where text will overlay. Gradient from dark (bottom) to golden light (top/center).

## Usage Notes

1. **No Text in Images:** All images should be free of text, captions, logos, or overlays. Typography will be added in design.

2. **Color Consistency:** Maintain the gold/dark color palette throughout. Golden highlights on dark backgrounds create the sophisticated feel.

3. **Emotional Tone:** Images should convey protection, guidance, permanence, and exclusivity — not flash or ostentation.

4. **Technical Quality:** All images should feel like medium-format photography — sharp where in focus, smooth bokeh elsewhere, high dynamic range.

5. **Contextual Use:**
   - Hero/Cover images for major section headers
   - Architecture & Legacy for About pages
   - Security & Protection for risk management services
   - Global Cities for international reach messaging
   - Private Estates for lifestyle messaging
   - Family & Multigenerational for wealth transfer topics
   - Abstract & Symbolic for transitions or conceptual sections
   - Business & Boardroom for professional services
